* "Concentration" field in samples, libraries, library aliquots, pool.
* "Parent ng Used" field in samples, libraries, library aliquots.
* "Initial Cell Concentration" in sample categories which are tissue processing, stock, aliquot (detailed samples).
* "Loading Cell Concentration" in sample categories which are tissue processing, stock, aliquot (detailed samples).
